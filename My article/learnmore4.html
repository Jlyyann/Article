CTU Canteen
A Guide to Responsible Dining

The school canteen is a place where we satisfy our hunger, share laughs with friends, and take a break from the day. To make it an enjoyable space for everyone, here’s a simple, thoughtful guide to buying food and cleaning up after yourself.

Start by Being Patient

If the canteen is busy, don’t rush in. Join the line and wait your turn. It’s only fair, and it keeps everything organized for everyone.

Pick Your Favorite Meal

Once it’s your turn, choose the food that catches your eye. Let the server know, and wait while they prepare it for you.

Pay for Your Food

Take your plate and head to the cashier. Be ready to pay, so the line moves smoothly, and others can get their meals faster.

Enjoy Your Meal

Find a table that feels right—maybe with your friends or just a quiet corner to relax. Savor your food, and take a moment to recharge.

Clean Up After Yourself

When you’re done eating, don’t just leave your mess behind. Throw away your trash in the bins provided. Take your plate to the sink and give it a quick rinse to remove leftover food. This small effort makes it easier for the dishwashers.

Place the Plate in the Dishwashing Area

Finally, put your rinsed plate in the designated basin. It’s a small gesture, but it shows respect for the hardworking canteen staff and keeps things running smoothly.

Taking care of your space in the canteen isn’t just about being neat—it’s about being part of a community that values kindness and respect. Let’s make the canteen a place everyone enjoys!
